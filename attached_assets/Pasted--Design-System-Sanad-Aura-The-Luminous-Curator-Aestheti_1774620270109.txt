# Design System: Sanad Aura
## The "Luminous Curator" Aesthetic Guide

This document defines the visual and technical foundations for the **Sanad National Health Intelligence Infrastructure**. It is designed to convey sovereignty, trust, and high-fidelity intelligence.

---

### 1. The Creative North Star: "The Luminous Curator"
Sanad rejects the flat, "utility-only" aesthetic of standard medical software. It is a **Decision System**, not a Data System. The visual language uses depth, light, and transparency to guide the eye toward critical life-saving actions.

---

### 2. Core Visual Principles
*   **Depth & Glassmorphism:** Use of `backdrop-blur` and translucent white/blue surfaces to create a sense of light-weight sophistication.
*   **Smooth Rounding:** A universal corner radius of **32px** for primary cards and **round-full** for buttons to emulate the premium feel of modern iOS interfaces.
*   **Forensic Typography:** Clear hierarchy with wide tracking for headers to ensure legibility under high-stress clinical environments.
*   **Decision-Centric Color:** Every color represents a clinical state, not just a decoration.

### 4. Typography (Inter / Public Sans)
*   **H1 (National Level):** `text-4xl font-black tracking-tighter text-slate-900`
*   **H2 (Clinical Context):** `text-2xl font-bold tracking-tight text-slate-800`
*   **Label (Institutional):** `text-xs font-bold uppercase tracking-widest text-slate-500`
*   **Body (Readability):** `text-base font-medium leading-relaxed text-slate-600`

### 6. Component Architecture (The "Sanad" Way)
1.  **The Priority Strip:** A full-width banner for critical alerts (e.g., Penicillin Allergy).
2.  **The Clinical Card:** White/70 background, 32px rounding, subtle 1px border.
3.  **The Decision Sidebar:** A dedicated vertical space for AI Insights and Actions.
4.  **The Audit Footer:** Persistent text showing data source and timestamps to build trust.

---

### 7. Implementation Guidelines
*   **Don't** use sharp corners.
*   **Don't** use high-saturation backgrounds for content areas.
*   **Always** provide a "Source" for data.
*   **Always** prioritize "Decision" over "Display".
